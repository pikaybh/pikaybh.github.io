    About Me

    I am a Ph.D. researcher at Seoul National University (SNU). I received my bachelor’s degree from Dankook University in 2023 and my master’s degree from SNU in 2025, respectively. I am currently located in Seoul, Korea, and work as a researcher in Seoul National University Construction Engineering & Management Lab. (SNUCEM).

    My research focuses on leveraging LLMs to automate tasks in construction engineering and management. Recently, I conducted research on this topic and published a paper.

    For more details, Please check the Projects and Publications section tabs!

    Apart from research, I enjoy crafting quirky gadgets, playing wargames, solving escape rooms, hiking, and occasionally reading.
